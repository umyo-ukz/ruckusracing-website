Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about racing with Ruckus

General Questions

To join Ruckus Racing:

  1. Create an account on our website
  2. Complete the rookie orientation and safety quiz
  3. Participate in practice sessions to establish your safety rating
  4. Register for leagues matching your skill level
  5. Join our Discord community for race communications

We currently support:

  • Assetto Corsa Competizione - Primary GT3/GT4 platform
  • Assetto Corsa - Various modded content and classic series
  • Le Mans Ultimate - Endurance and prototype racing

We are continuously evaluating additional simulators based on community interest.

Basic participation in our rookie and amateur leagues is completely free! We offer:

  • Free Tier: Access to rookie leagues, basic stats, and community events
  • Premium Tier ($5/month): Pro leagues, advanced telemetry, priority registration, and exclusive content
  • Team Packages: Discounted rates for racing teams

Racing & Competition

Our safety rating system tracks your driving behavior across multiple factors:

  • Incident Points: Contact with other cars, off-tracks, and corner cutting
  • Consistency: Lap time variation and control
  • Race Craft: Overtaking, defending, and spatial awareness
  • Completion Rate: Finishing races without major incidents

Ratings range from Rookie (R) to Professional (P1-P5). Higher ratings unlock more competitive leagues.

We have a comprehensive incident review system:

  • Live Stewards: Real-time incident monitoring during races
  • Post-Race Review: Detailed analysis of reported incidents
  • Penalty System: Time penalties, points deductions, or race bans
  • Appeal Process: Opportunity to contest steward decisions

All incidents are reviewed within 24 hours of race completion.

Technical Support

Minimum requirements vary by simulator:

Assetto Corsa Competizione:

Windows 10, Intel i5-4460 or AMD FX-8120, GTX 760 or R9 270X, 8GB RAM

General Requirements:

Stable internet connection (10Mbps+), steering wheel recommended, Discord for communication

For technical support:

  • Discord: #tech-support channel for immediate help
  • Email: support@ruckusracing.com for detailed issues
  • Race Control: Contact stewards during active sessions

Please include your system specs, error logs, and steps to reproduce the issue.
