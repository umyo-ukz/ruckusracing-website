About Ruckus Racing

Creating chaos and competition in the sim racing world since 2023

Our Story

Ruckus Racing League was born from a simple idea: sim racing should be accessible, competitive, and most importantly - fun. What started as a small group of friends racing on weekends has evolved into one of the most vibrant sim racing communities.

We believe in fair competition, clean racing, and creating an environment where both beginners and pros can thrive. Our live stewarding system and professional broadcast team ensure every race feels like the real thing.
